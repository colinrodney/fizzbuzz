FizzBuzz Challenge

Write a program that prints the numbers from 1 to 100. For numberss that are multiples of three print "Fizz" instead of the number and for numbers that are multiples of five print "Buzz". For numbers which are multiples of both three and five print "FizzBuzz"

Click 'fizzbuzz' button to run program then scroll to the bottom and click the 'reset' button to reload the page.
